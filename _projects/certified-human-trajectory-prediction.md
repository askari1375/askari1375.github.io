This project introduced a certification framework for human trajectory prediction, adapting randomized smoothing to regression and multi-agent trajectory forecasting.

My contributions included implementing and evaluating certification components, integrating a diffusion-based denoiser to recover accuracy and tighten bounds, and helping design certified metrics such as Certified-ADE, Certified-FDE, Certified Collision Rate, ABD, and FBD.

The work was accepted to CVPR 2025 {% cite bahari2025certified %}. See the [publication](/publications/) for the paper link.
