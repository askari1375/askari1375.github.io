At Disney Research Studios Zurich, I contributed to a research direction on synthetic and semi-synthetic pretraining for neural networks and diffusion models.

The project studied improved neural fractals and reverse stylization: transferring visual features from a small set of license-free real images onto synthetic images to reduce the domain gap. My work focused on diffusion-related experiments, evaluation design, and attention-map similarity as one way to compare synthetic and real-data pretraining behavior.
